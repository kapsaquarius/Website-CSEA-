Timeline

Valar Doubtulis

8 Sept 2019

The purpose of this event is to teach those topics in PSCP where the first year students might face difficulty in understanding and also to break the ice between seniors and the freshers. The event not only focussed on clearing doubts but also teaching new topics which might be helpful in inculcating an interest in coding.

DSA Session

8 Sept 2019

Be it projects or interviews, Data Structures are one of the core essentials for a CSE student. It is the one subject which haunts every student and to make things easier, CSEA came up with the event on DS Learning. All concepts of Data Structures were discussed from the scratch for better understanding. The sessions were open to all branches.

12 Sept 2019

CSEA Freshers’

An event exclusively for B.Tech and MCA freshers to break the ice between seniors and juniors, and to know their future peers well and enjoy an evening of fun.

Devathon

14 Sept 2019

CSEA and WSDC, organized DEVATHON 2019 on 14th September from 7pm onwards, a first-of-a-kind hackathon in NITW where people from all years of study formed groups and worked on fun ideas over a period of 24 hours- enabling students to learn from each other, and apply the knowledge to make something cool.

Meme Making

2 Oct 2019

Setting and keeping up with trends is what we do at CSEA and the 2.0 edition of MEME MAKING bears testimony to that. The event not only received hilarious responses but was also one was of the most successful online event of all times. The judges were our online audience and the winning entry was adjudged based on the most number of reacts.

404 Resolved

4 Oct 2019

We will be featuring modules of web development, machine learning, basic coding and competitive programming, and blockchains. The list doesn't stop there! We will also be showcasing the different opportunities ranging from research to GSOC.

7 Oct 2019

T-shirt Designing

CSEA launched the "T-Shirt Design Contest" in search of some designing talent among students. The number of responses received were quite huge, making it difficult for the judges to choose the best design.

Codeathon 1.0

10 Oct 2019

Just like reading and writing, coding has become a life-time skill to be acquired in today’s world. In order to inculcate the competitive coding culture in the campus, we bring to you Codeathon, a series of online competitive programming contests where the participants were given 5-6 questions to be programmed and submitted within 3 hours.

18 Oct 2019

Know Your Branch

The event will deal with brief insights on the different avenues CSE, as a branch, has to offer including internships, placements, study tips and research opportunities. The students will be told about various prestigious programmes that happen globally including ACM-ICPC, GSoC, etc.

19 Oct 2019

Internship Tales

An initiative to help the current 3rd year students, in answering their queries about the respective internships which they would be undertaking in the summer of 2020. Respective Final Year students who had just completed their internships come forward to satiate the curious minds.

22 Oct 2019

Talk on Foreign & Research Internships

An event to make the students aware about the opportunities in research and foreign internships. The students will be formed about all the programmes that they can be a part, for each year.
